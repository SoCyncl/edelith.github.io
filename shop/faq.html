Returns & FAQ

Answers to common questions — shipping, returns, and all the fine print.

◆ Shipping ◆
Processing Time

Orders are typically fulfilled within 3–7 business days after purchase. Expect delays during high-volume periods.

International Shipping

We ship worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes charged upon arrival — these are the buyer's responsibility.

Delivery Estimates

Domestic: 7–14 days. International: 14–30 days. Expedited options may be available at checkout.

Order Tracking

You'll receive a tracking link by email once your order has shipped. Check your spam folder if it hasn't arrived after a few days.

◆ Returns ◆
Can I return or exchange an item? +

We accept returns on defective or damaged items only. If your order arrived damaged, please contact us within 14 days of delivery with photos of the damage. We'll arrange a replacement or refund. Items in good condition cannot be returned unless there was an error in fulfillment.

My order arrived damaged — what do I do? +

We're sorry to hear that! Please contact us with your order number and clear photos of the damaged item and packaging. We'll get you sorted as quickly as possible — usually within 2–3 business days.

I ordered the wrong size — can I exchange it? +

Unfortunately, size exchanges are not guaranteed as items are printed-on-demand and each order is unique. Please check the size guide on each product page before ordering. If you're unsure, feel free to reach out before placing your order.

How long do refunds take to process? +

Once a refund is approved, it typically takes 5–10 business days to appear on your statement depending on your bank or card provider.

◆ Orders & Products ◆
Are items print-on-demand? +

Yes! Items in The Emporium are fulfilled through Fourthwall, meaning each piece is produced when you order it. This ensures quality and allows us to offer a wide range of products without maintaining physical inventory.

Can I cancel my order? +

Orders can typically be cancelled within 24 hours of placement, before they enter production. After that window, cancellation is not guaranteed. Please contact us immediately if you need to cancel.

Why does my tracking show no movement? +

Tracking updates can sometimes lag behind the actual movement of your package, especially for international shipments. If your tracking hasn't updated in over 10 business days, please contact us and we'll investigate.

Do you offer discounts or promo codes? +

Occasionally! Keep an eye on the Solstitheo Archives homepage for announcements about limited-time offers tied to campaign milestones, session anniversaries, and special events.
