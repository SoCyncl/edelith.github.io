Session 11

Tarpaul Pit

Blobert and Herb find themselves stuck in a dark maze. From above, they can hear Peggle’s voice guiding them through the puzzle, all while being chased by a giant[[ corrosion droplets]]. After many hours—and with Blobert almost dying—they manage to solve the maze and teleport to a separate room, where the magical mist clears. Exhausted, they decide to sleep in the cavern.

While Peggle is keeping watch, he encounters Baylis, who asks if they’ve collected the [[Edelith Pods]] and then leaves. In the morning, the party notices their[[ Edelith Pods]] are gone and finds a note from Baylis telling them to "meet him at his lady's temple."

They continue exploring until they find a separate Dodecahedron guarded by [[Crystalline Husk]] and their [[Crystalline Horse Husk]] and place it on a pedestal, which unlocks a door. Inside, they find Mr. V, who reveals that he took the [[Edelith Pods]] and plans to raid Edelith the next day. He explains that he wants to kill General Strom to take his place and make Tarpaul Pit free of corrosion through any means necessary—even if it means killing those who oppose him. The party refuses to help him.

Session 1

Shroud Crossers

Blobert, Herb, Peggle, and Eight wait in the ruins of a sandy shelter for their guide, Charon. When Charon arrives, he shows the party his metallic hand, which he says is the key to breaking through the Shroud. Just then, Charon's hand begins to glow and sear with pain. He falls to the ground, clutching his hand, and tells the party that their way of entering the Shroud is near—they must leave now. As they cross the desert through a sandstorm, Charon suddenly senses something approaching. The ground beneath them starts to rumble, and a giant arrow whistles past the party, clearing the sandstorm and piercing the Shroud. Just then, a massive mechanical demon with a humanoid form tumbles down the dunes, rushes toward the arrow, and starts clawing at the Shroud, tearing a hole in it.

Charon shouts at the party, telling them that this is their only chance to get through the Shroud. The party runs toward the hole in the Shroud. Suddenly, the sand begins to swirl like whirlpools beneath their feet. Multiple scavengers—raiders who prey on travelers—appear from beneath the swirling sand. As a fight breaks out, Herb gives his mask to Peggle. Peggle rages, while Blobert slices off a piece of his slime to make a turret, and Eight fires out Chaos Bolts. The party manages to take down two scavengers; however, two more gang up on Peggle. Their attacks bounce off of him, so they target his mask, and they manage to crack it. Through the crack, the scavenger manages to catch a glimpse of Peggle's bloodshot eyes staring back at him. Before the scavenger can react, Peggle slices one in half. The other tries to flee, but Peggle catches up and decapitates him.

Before the last scavanger dies, he screams something in their language. Moments later, more scavengers appear, led by their leader, emerging near the tear in the Shroud. Charon yells at the party to hurry before the tear closes. They all sprint toward the opening, but Blobert comes face to face with the scavenger leader, who slices into him three times, cutting away at his slime. Blobert manages to retreat, allowing Peggle and Charon to fight the leader. During the fight, Charon slips and falls, leaving Peggle and Eight to fight off the leader and the remaining scavengers. Meanwhile, Herb heals Blobert while the fight continues.

In the distance, Blobert's turret, which had been slowly making its way up, fires a projectile. The projectile flies through the sand, the sheer heat and speed of the projectile causing the grains of sand around it to melt into tiny shards of glass. It whizzes past Peggle and strikes the scavenger leader straight in the chest, knocking him back and engulfing him in a cloud of smoke and sand, killing him on impact. The last scavenger attempts to run away. Herb calmly walks up to him, staring him down without saying a word. As the scavenger freezes because of Herb's gaze, Blobert's turret fires one last shot, whizzing past Herb's head and striking the scavenger.

After they defeat the scavengers, they thank Charon for his help before entering the Shroud. Blinded by the brightness of the Shroud, their eyes take a moment to adjust. When they do, they find themselves in a peaceful chapel. Just as they begin to take in their surroundings, they hear a snarling sound coming from behind them. The mechanical demon from earlier has breached the Shroud and is now demanding a challenge.

Suddenly, a glowing spark brighter than the stars, brighter than even the sun itself, flies down from above, accompanied by a single, synthetic note sung by an angelic choir. Descending from the light is a celestial, a being so powerful it makes the earth shudder when it lands. The celestial locks eyes with the demon, and the two forces prepare to attack. The party is now locked in a battle between a demonic archer and an angelic guardian.
