Summary

Not much is known of Vaskal Krastov, other than he leads the Iron Fang Sect in Bjornguard. He and Peggle had a previous fight that peggle had won, and he is out for his blood once again. He has with him a young huntress who erratically demanded help when the party had arrived to a monestary in the mountains. Once Peggle made his name clear, Vaskal in a blinding action quickly stood ready to attack. The entire party ran away.

Personality

Vaskal in his initial appearance did not speak until the very end, he has a cold, serious and intimidating personality, he acts before he speaks, and is methodical and unusually cruel.
