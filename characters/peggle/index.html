Summary

Peggle Krastov is a Goliath, Bear Totem Barbarian from the North countries. In search of inheritance from his noble family, and for the sake of his niece, he consulted the best anger management in Solstitheo, inside the Immortal City of Edelith.

Personality

Peggle is a kind, but hardy soul that refuses to yield and bow to anyone aside from family. The only thing stopping him is his own anger and temperment and now seeks a therapist who can help him control his anger, so he may be the best Uncle to his neice.
