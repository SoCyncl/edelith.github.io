Summary

Baxter Kokayin is a Wood Gnome Wizard from a far away land. He was raised in a noble family. Baxter was taught in a religious school that focused on Conjuration. He was praised by both his classmates and teachers for being a natural prodigy. Once he graduated the school, he went out on an adventure as a freeman, exploring the outer world and wishing goodbye to his family and friends back in his forest hometown.

Personality

Despite his natural look, he is very clueless, interrogative, and question-oriented about all things that happen.
