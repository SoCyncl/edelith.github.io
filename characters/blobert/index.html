Summary

Blobert Goopington is a Plasmoid Artificer from an unknown tower. He was made in a lab by a wizard who left him there after The Wake of Steel. He ran out, seeing chickens also running out, and shape-shifted after them and followed until he was found and viciously beaten by Herb Realboyington who named and began to travel with him.

Personality

Blobert is oblivious, childlike and wondourous; surviving in a word far too harsh for him, and far too complex for him to understand. Maybe it's that reckless curiosity that let's him avoid the true horrors of the world and continue living like nothing ever happens.
