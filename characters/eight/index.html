Summary

Eight Nakano is a human, Divine Soul Sorcerer, with an unknown backstory, is at a crossroad in life, and the desire to see the world and find his place in it, so he seeks The Immortal City of Edelith for answers, journey, for something that lets him feel alive.

Personality

Eight is a very mellow, and chill guy, all jokes aside. He goes wherever the wind blows him and whatever option he thinks is best in the moment.
