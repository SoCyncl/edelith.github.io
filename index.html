Solstitheo Archives is a fan project documenting the original DnD story "The Immortal City of Edelith" created by Horkah. This site holds all the lore, sessions, characters, and oddities — for safekeeping, and as proof that we all did something together.

Please enjoy your stay.

Seversova Arc ◆ Session 20

Latest Session

Full Archive →

Gambling. Gambling. Gambling. The group used Peggle's drinking contest winnings to triple their coin. Peggle met a tall Goliath he wanted to impress, while Herb discovered the tragic truth of his kind — the Woodsworn, betrayed by Rotcio, who sold every last one of his kin for the promise of immortality. Ringo, a surviving Woodsworn, laid it all bare. Eventually, the group made for the mountains and sheltered in a cave.

Key Events
  • Gambling — repeatedly and with enthusiasm.
  • Peggle meets a tall Goliath rival at the tables.
  • Herb learns the tragedy of the Woodsworn people.
  • Rotcio revealed as the traitor who sold his kin.
  • The party takes shelter in a mountain cave.
Open Threads
  • What drove Rotcio to betray his people?
  • What lies further up the mountain?
  • Will Peggle impress the mystery Goliath?

Wake of Steel
An event in Solstitheian history where swarms of metallic demons emerged from fissures in the earth, seeking to consume everything with their Corrosion.

Woodsworn
A race of wooden constructs created by and adhering to the god Hiebrief — identifiable by their humanoid appearance, wooden plating, and mask-like faces.
Read more →
Shroud of Aurum
The conic barrier of light cast by the Circlet of the Sun encompassing all of Edelith, granting immortality to select creatures within its boundary.

A battalion of metal-wielding soldiers in Post-Wake Edelith promoting repulsion of demons and regional stability. Known for their Thunderstroke weaponry.
